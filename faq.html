General Info.

Be My Senses is a revolutionary online platform that seamlessly integrates American Sign Language (ASL) interpretation with advanced multimedia features, catering to the diverse communication needs of individuals with hearing impairments. At the heart of this innovative platform is an ASL Interpreter, proficient in translating spoken language into clear and expressive sign language through live video streaming.
